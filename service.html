Teeth braces are orthodontic devices used to straighten misaligned teeth and improve bite. They work by applying gentle pressure over time to gradually shift teeth into proper alignment. Benefits include enhanced dental aesthetics, improved bite functionality, reduced risk of dental issues like cavities or gum disease, easier cleaning and maintenance, and increased confidence with a beautiful smile. Braces can effectively address overcrowding, spacing issues, and bite irregularities, leading to long-term oral health improvements.

A root canal is a dental procedure that involves removing infected or damaged pulp from within the tooth, cleaning, disinfecting, and sealing it. Benefits include alleviating severe tooth pain, preserving the natural tooth, preventing the spread of infection to surrounding areas, restoring normal biting and chewing function, and maintaining the tooth's structural integrity, avoiding extraction in many cases.

Dental implants are artificial tooth roots surgically placed into the jawbone to support replacement teeth or bridges. Benefits include restored functionality for biting and chewing, enhanced aesthetics providing a natural look, improved speech, prevention of bone loss in the jaw, and long-term durability, often lasting a lifetime with proper care. They offer a stable, permanent solution for missing teeth without compromising neighboring teeth.
